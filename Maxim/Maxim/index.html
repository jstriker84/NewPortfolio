Who am I?

I chase creativity

After earning a Master's degree in Spanish literature, Jillian has contracted for some of the most well-known tech companies in Austin, such as Facebook and Apple. She now ventures into the world of web design. She is a quick study and has learned how to use a variety of computer systems on both Mac OS and Windows during her time as a contract editor. She learned how to use html, CSS, Bootstrap and more front-end processes, all of which will be demoed both in the structure of this website. She is also familiar with Javascript, SQL, MongoDB, React, and React Native.

Jillian is an expert editor in English and Spanish; she has written her own blog for the past 3 years and is looking forward to expanding into the professional world. This portfolio showcases her work as both a freelance web developer and writer.
